TEAM PROCEDURES

  • Regular meetings will be held weekly in UH142, Friday 11.15-12.05pm. When necessary, additional meetings will be scheduled through GroupMe.
  • Communication will mainly be through GroupMe, especially for scheduling meetings and providing progress updates.
  • Decisions will be made by majority and in the case of a tie, will be decided by the current team lead.
  • The currently assigned team leader will set the agenda for all team meetings. They shall also remind team members of upcoming meetings and provide the meeting’s agenda through the shared Google Drive repository ahead of time. The team leader shall ensure the team stays on track during meetings and if all items in the agenda for that meeting can not be addressed in the allotted time, the meeting shall be extended or the items not addressed shall receive higher priority for the following meeting.
  • Meeting minutes will be taken at every meeting, and the team member responsible for doing so will rotate every meeting. Said minutes will be kept in the shared Google Drive repository. The minutes shall be uploaded within a reasonable amount of time and necessarily before the next team meeting.

TEAM EXPECTATIONS

  • Work Quality:
  • Each member agrees to produce the best work they can and in a timely fashion. All work will be peer reviewed by the rest of the team before any deadline submission.
  • Individuals and current team lead must fulfill their responsibilities at least one day before the deadline or notify the team in advance.
  • Team Participation:
  • Strategies to ensure cooperation and equal distribution of tasks:
  • Current team leader will assign and monitor all tasks in progress.
  • Strategies for encouraging/including ideas from all team members (team maintenance):
  • Every member will have an opportunity at the start of each meeting to voice their opinions, thoughts, and concerns on the project.
  • Strategies for keeping on task (task maintenance):
  • Team members will hold each other accountable for any ongoing tasks, and each member will update the rest of the team on their progress towards completing their assigned task.
  • Personal Accountability:
  • Members shall attend all meetings, excluding extraneous circumstances. All members shall be on time to every meeting or provide notice in a timely manner. All members are expected to participate and contribute to the best of their ability.
  • Each member is responsible for completing their assigned task in a timely manner or communicating with the rest of the team if this is not possible.
  • Each member is responsible for communicating with all other team members regarding any issues that may arise. Team members are expected to communicate honestly and frequently with each other.
  • Team members are expected to fully commit to the assigned project and perform to the best of their abilities at all times.
  • Consequences for Failing to Follow Procedures and Fulfill Expectations:
  • If a member does not meet these standards, the issue will first be addressed at a team meeting, where the team leader (or another member if team leader is the subject of discussion) will be responsible for handling the issue.
  • If problem persists, the team can handle the situation on a case-by-case basis.

TEAM LEADERSHIP

Every person on the team will assume the role of team leader for two weeks, operating on a bi-weekly rotation. The role of the team leader will be to organize meetings, set agendas, resolve team conflicts, and ensure work meets team standards and is submitted in a timely manner.
